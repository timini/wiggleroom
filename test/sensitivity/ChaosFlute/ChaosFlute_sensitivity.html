Interpretation Guide

MetricMeaning
Sensitivity ScoreOverall measure of parameter impact (0-1). Higher = more effect on sound.
CV (Coefficient of Variation)Relative spread of feature values. Higher = more variation.
Correlation (r)Linear relationship with parameter. +1/-1 = strong, 0 = none.

LOW IMPACT (score < 0.1): Parameter may not be working or has subtle effect.
MEDIUM (0.1 - 0.3): Parameter has noticeable effect.
HIGH IMPACT (score > 0.3): Parameter significantly changes the sound.
